Polar H10 — extract & download ECG / RR

Capture the H10's raw ECG (130 Hz, µV, via the Polar Measurement Data service) and/or RR intervals (via the Heart Rate service), then download them. Chrome/Edge only.

Everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded. The .txt RR file is one interval (ms) per line — drop it straight into tools/hrv_run.py or the WASM core.
Note: this captures the live stream while you record. The H10 can also record to its own internal memory (offline recording), but that read-out is an AES-encrypted Polar-SDK protocol not reimplemented here. Web Bluetooth needs Chrome/Edge over https or localhost. Research tool — not a medical device.
