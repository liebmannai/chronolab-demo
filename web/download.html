Nothing leaves your browser. Per its firmware, the sensor sends its stored recording as a JSON stream; this tool concatenates the frame payloads and lets you save them, then analyse the JSON. If it doesn't complete, the raw log shows the exact bytes — send it back (or an Android Bluetooth HCI snoop log of the official app downloading) and the exact protocol can be implemented. Web Bluetooth needs Chrome/Edge over https or localhost. Research tool — not a medical device.
